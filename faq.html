What to know before starting a painting project.

A good painting project starts with clear expectations. Here are the common questions homeowners ask before hiring JDV Custom Painting.

Painting Process

Common questions

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most interior painting projects take 1 to 3 days, depending on the number of rooms, wall condition, prep work, and drying time. Larger homes or full exterior projects may take several days or longer. JDV Custom Painting will give you a clear timeline before starting so you know what to expect.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, JDV Custom Painting offers free estimates. We’ll review the space, ask a few questions, and explain what the job will involve. After that, you’ll receive a clear price based on the size of the project, prep work, and materials needed.

Do you help with color selection?

Yes. If you already have colors picked, we can work with those. If you need help, we can give practical suggestions based on lighting, room size, trim color, and the overall look you want. The goal is to help you choose colors that look great in your actual home, not just on a sample card.

What is included in the painting process?

A proper painting job includes more than just applying paint. We prepare the area, protect floors and furniture, patch minor wall imperfections, sand where needed, apply primer if required, and then paint with clean, even coats. Good prep work is what makes the finished job look professional and last longer.

Do you move furniture?

We can help move light furniture away from the walls when needed. Larger or delicate items should be moved by the homeowner before the project starts. Everything that stays in the room is covered and protected before painting begins.

Do you repair drywall before painting?

Yes, minor drywall repairs can usually be handled before painting. This may include nail holes, small dents, cracks, and rough spots. Larger drywall damage may require additional repair work, which can be reviewed during the estimate.

How many coats of paint are included?

Most projects include two coats for a clean, even finish. Some colors, surfaces, or major color changes may require primer or additional coats. JDV Custom Painting will explain this before the job starts so there are no surprises.

What paint brands do you use?

We can work with trusted paint brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, Behr, or another brand preferred by the customer. The right paint depends on the room, surface, finish, and budget. We’ll recommend a good option that makes sense for the project.

Do you paint both interiors and exteriors?

Yes, JDV Custom Painting handles both interior and exterior painting. Interior work may include walls, ceilings, trim, doors, cabinets, and accent walls. Exterior work may include siding, trim, doors, fences, decks, and other outdoor surfaces.

How do you protect the home during painting?

Floors, furniture, fixtures, and nearby surfaces are covered before painting begins. Tape, plastic, drop cloths, and proper prep materials are used to keep the work area clean. At the end of the job, the area is cleaned up so the home feels finished, not messy.

Can we stay in the house while painting is being done?

Yes, in most cases you can stay in the home while interior painting is being completed. We work area by area to keep the project manageable. If strong odors, sanding, or larger work is involved, we’ll let you know what to expect ahead of time.

How soon can I use the room after painting?

Most painted rooms can be used the same day once the paint is dry to the touch. However, paint continues curing for several days, so it’s best to be careful with touching walls, moving furniture back tightly, or cleaning the surface right away. We’ll let you know when the space is ready.

Do you clean up after the job?

Yes. Cleanup is part of the job. We remove tape, coverings, materials, and leave the area neat. The goal is for the customer to enjoy the finished space without dealing with a mess afterward.

How do I schedule a project?

You can call, text, or email JDV Custom Painting to schedule an estimate. Send photos if you have them, or request an in-person visit. For quotes, email info@jdvcustompainting.com.
