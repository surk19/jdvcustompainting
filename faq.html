Frequently Asked Questions

Everything homeowners usually ask before starting a painting project.

JDV Custom Painting believes a great project starts with clear expectations and good communication.

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most interior painting projects take 1 to 3 days depending on room size, prep work, and drying time. Larger projects or exterior work may take several more days. We always provide a realistic timeline before starting the project.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. JDV Custom Painting provides free estimates for residential and commercial projects. We’ll review the project details, answer your questions, and provide a straightforward quote with no pressure.

Do you help with color selection?

Absolutely. If needed, we can help guide customers toward colors that work well with lighting, flooring, trim, and the overall feel of the home. The goal is to help you choose colors that look good long term, not just on a paint sample.

Do you repair drywall before painting?

Minor drywall repairs such as nail holes, cracks, dents, and small imperfections are usually repaired before painting. Larger repairs can also be discussed during the estimate process.

How many coats of paint are included?

Most projects include two coats for proper coverage and a professional finish. Certain colors or surfaces may require additional coats or primer depending on the condition of the walls.

Do you move furniture?

We help move light furniture away from walls when necessary. Floors and furniture are protected before painting begins to keep the home clean and safe throughout the project.

What paint brands do you use?

We commonly work with Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, Behr, and other trusted paint brands. Paint recommendations are based on durability, finish quality, and the customer’s budget.

Do you paint interiors and exteriors?

Yes. JDV Custom Painting handles both interior and exterior painting projects including walls, trim, ceilings, siding, doors, fences, decks, and more.

How do you protect the home during painting?

Floors, furniture, fixtures, and surrounding areas are covered and protected before work begins. Clean preparation is a major part of delivering a professional finished result.

How do I schedule a project?

You can call, text, or email JDV Custom Painting directly to request a free estimate. Email: info@jdvcustompainting.com
